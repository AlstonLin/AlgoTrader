About Us

       Algorithmic trading, also know as black-block trading or algo trading, refers to the manner in which we can program computers to follow a predetermined set of instructures to place automated trade requests. Sophisicated algoirthms are not only able to more accurately predict the short-term trends of the stock market, but also eclipse the rate human traders are capable of processing information. Algorithmic trading firms such as Two Sigma, are capable of manipulating these algorithms to allow them to generate profit at seemingly impossible speeds and frequencies.

        AlgoTrader is a educational tool for algorithmic traders and any finance enthusiasts. Through AlgoTrader, users are able to make create an algorithm to buy and sell stocks based on historical data, pulled from Nasdaq's API.
